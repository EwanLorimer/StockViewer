Primary objectives:

User can search for a stock ticker, retrieve current price as well as graph historic prices
User can change timeframe for historic prices (e.g. 1 month, 3 months, 1 year or even between 2 dates)
User can have a profile with saved stocks and a portfolio
Display stock info such as dividend yield (and percentage), P/E, exchange listed on,

Multiple user accounts
Secondary Objectives:
Provide data for other indicators such as RSI
